Introduction

Welcome to StockHawk - a free resource for stock market enthusiasts. Created by two juniors at Argyle High School, Abhinav Vittalam & Tanay Munugala, StockHawk is designed to provide valuable insights & tools for better financial decision-making. We're here to make the stock market accessible & understandable for everyone.

Our Purpose

We want to provide straighforward, useful information about the stock market. We want to help you make informed financial decisions with easy-to-understand analysis and tools. We made the website to learn how to analyze & invest in stocks properly & just want to share what we learned with others to make their journey easier than ours was. StockHawk is just a passion project that combines Tanay & I's interest in programming & finance so bear with us if the website isn't easy to use or you feel values you are given (particularly by the DCF Analysis [Beta]) aren't accurate. In any case, we hope you find the website helpful & easy to use/understand.
